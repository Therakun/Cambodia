Welcome to Cambodia!

Cambodia is a Southeastern Asian nation whose landscape spans low-lying plains, The Mekong Delta, mountains and the Gulf of Thailand coastline. The capital, Phnom Penh, is home to the art deco Central Market, glittering Royal Palace and the National Museum's historical and archaeological exhibits. In the country's northwest are the ruins of Angkor Wat, a massive stone temple complex built during the Khmer Empire.
